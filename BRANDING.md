# Robot Heart Branding Guidelines

## Brand Identity

**Robot Heart** is an iconic art car and music platform at Burning Man, known for its distinctive robot character with a heart made of dots/circles.

## Visual Identity

### Logo
- Minimalist robot character with rounded rectangular body
- Heart pattern made of circular dots on the chest
- Simple geometric shapes (rectangles, circles)
- Antenna on top of head
- Square eyes and straight-line mouth
- Rounded arms extending from sides

### Color Palette

**Primary Colors:**
- Cream/Beige (#E8DCC8) - Robot body color
- Dark Brown (#3D2817) - Robot features (eyes, mouth, heart dots)
- Warm Earth Tones - Background (desert/playa environment)

**Accent Colors (from playa environment):**
- Sunset Orange (#FF6B35)
- Dusty Pink (#FF8B94)
- Turquoise Blue (#4ECDC4)
- Golden Yellow (#FFD23F)

**UI Colors:**
- Background: Dark mode with warm grays (#2A2A2A, #1A1A1A)
- Accent: Warm orange/coral for CTAs
- Text: Cream/off-white for primary text

### Typography
- Clean, modern sans-serif fonts
- Geometric letterforms that echo the robot's aesthetic
- Suggested: SF Pro (iOS native), or geometric sans like Futura, Avenir

### Design Language
- **Minimalist & Geometric**: Simple shapes, clean lines
- **Warm & Approachable**: Despite being a robot, it has heart
- **Playful yet Functional**: Fun but tactical/utilitarian
- **Desert/Playa Aesthetic**: Warm earth tones, dust, sunset colors

## App Design Principles

1. **Dark Mode First**: Playa environment = bright sun, dark UI reduces eye strain
2. **Big Touch Targets**: Dusty fingers, gloves, one-handed operation
3. **High Contrast**: Readable in bright sunlight
4. **Smooth Animations**: Reflect the fluid, musical nature of Robot Heart
5. **Heartbeat Motif**: Use pulsing/beating animations for connection status
